Who are we?

Created on September 1, 2018, DakarOxy is a citizen-initiated project that stemmed from the desire to awaken our fellow countrymen and encourage the greening of our cities in hope to counter the terrible effects of air pollution. Our objective? To help reforest our country, in the outskirts of Dakar and in the regions alike.

As a small team who works with mostly personal funding and on the side of our professional activities, our reach remains humble, and the campaigns we run, modest. Nevertheless driven by strong patriotic sentiments, we believe in the value of every deed, big or small.

Our hope to curb the ecological crisis due to global warming, leaving a cleaner earth for future generations, might be ambitious, but our strength lies in numbers: if we all work together, no challenge will be able to resist us.

PatriOxy

Since the beginning of its creation, DakarOxy's primary goal has been to encourage people to green their neighborhood by helping them understand the vital role that trees play. Senegal has more sixteen million inhabitants, and if everyone were to do their share today, then our country would over sixteen million trees richer, which would arguably help thwart the consequences of the toxic greenhouse gases that threaten our health and the Earth.

People of Senegal, join us in the fight against this ecological crisis whose scope goes well beyond borders.

Campaigns

Among DakarOxy's recurring activities, we run reforestation campaigns in the most tree-depleted neighborhoods in the outskirts of Dakar, as well as in the regions.

These are carried out following the request and with the help of local populations after a contract of commitment to protect and water the trees has been established between DakarOxy and the beneficiaries. This is a sine qua non condition for any campaign to optimize the trees' chances of survival while they reach maturity and autonomy.

Check out the work we do in the field on our Instagram account and YouTube channel.

EcolOxy

As bringing awareness to the youth is fundamental to help them preserve their environment, one of DakarOxy's mission is to intervene directly within schools to sensitize our children to the problems that come with air pollution, including global warming. As adults of tomorrow, our children must be active today to help us solve a problem that concerns our generations as much as those to come.

Moral and Physical Contract

Because we are convinced of the importance of patriotic initiatives, we try to do more than just plant trees: we meet the people who benefit from our campaigns after they have submitted a request, and work with them to green their neighborhoods. A moral and physical contract is drawn to concretize the commitment of both parties.

Conditions

Reforestation requests must meet a couple of prerequisites to be considered by the team:

  • being an association or a group of people committed to monitoring and maintaining the trees
  • making a request for a reforestation campaign by sending us a message on our Facebook page
  • organizing a meeting where the trees will be planted

DakarOxy's Commitment

If the request is approved by the team, DakarOxy then agrees to:

  • provide trees, fencing, fertilizer
  • come by regularly, either during a planned visit or unannounced, until the trees are autonomous, in order to evaluate their good development
  • cover the campaign and its follow-ups

The Commitment of the Beneficiaries

In return, the beneficiaries of the campaign must commit to:

  • establish a list of recipients who will contribute to the tree with the symbolic sum of 500 FCFA (0.76€) per registered person
  • dig the holes for the trees at least 24 hours before the campaign
  • ensure daily watering and the protection of the trees
  • regularly check the health of the trees
  • designate a liaison agent who will periodically report to DakarOxy on the progress of the trees, with the help of pictures
  • replace the tree in case of disappearance
